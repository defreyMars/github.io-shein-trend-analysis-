Privacy Policy for SHEIN Internal Trend Radar

Organization: SHEIN (Global Fashion & Lifestyle Retailing)

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how the internal data analysis tool, developed for SHEIN, manages information accessed via the Pinterest API. This tool is for internal research only.

2. Data Collection & Usage

The Tool does NOT collect personal identifiable information (PII). We only access aggregated trend metrics to inform SHEIN's internal design decisions.

3. Compliance

We strictly adhere to Pinterest Developer Terms. SHEIN does not sell or share any data obtained from the Pinterest API with third parties.
