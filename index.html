I · Overview

Executive Summary

This platform transforms Pinterest's global search trend signals into actionable supply chain decisions for SHEIN's design team. By applying a proprietary quantitative scoring model to publicly available trend keyword data, we identify high-signal trend categories weeks before they reach mainstream market saturation — enabling precision inventory planning and product development prioritization.

All data processed by this platform is derived exclusively from aggregated, anonymized, publicly available trend metadata. No personally identifiable information is collected, stored, or transmitted at any stage of the pipeline.

II · Methodology

Quantitative Methodology

Each trend keyword is evaluated on two independent axes: Exploding Factor (B) measures short-term search velocity and burst intensity; Growth Rate (G) measures sustained longitudinal growth momentum. The composite Intensity Score (S) is computed as:

S  =  0.6 × B  +  0.4 × G
B  =  Exploding Factor   ·   G  =  Growth Rate   ·   S  =  Signal Intensity
Algorithm · Core Scoring Logic
def compute_intensity(burst_factor, growth_rate): # Weighted composite of burst velocity and sustained growth weighted_burst = burst_factor * 0.6 weighted_growth = growth_rate * 0.4 return round(weighted_burst + weighted_growth)

The 0.6 / 0.4 weighting reflects empirical observation that burst velocity is a stronger leading indicator of short-term supply chain relevance than sustained growth alone.

IV · Compliance

Privacy Policy & Compliance Declaration

For Pinterest API Review

1. Overview

SHEIN Trends Internal Radar for Designer ONLY is an internal research and analysis tool developed exclusively for SHEIN's design and trend intelligence team. Its sole purpose is to support internal market research by analyzing publicly available trend keyword data. Access is strictly limited to authorized SHEIN designers and trend analysts. This tool is not a consumer-facing product and is not accessible to the general public.

2. Data We Access

This tool accesses only the following categories of data:

(1) Publicly available trend keyword signals (e.g., trending search terms and categories);
(2) Aggregated, anonymized market trend metadata provided by third-party platform APIs.

We do NOT collect, process, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) of Pinterest users or any other individuals. No usernames, emails, profile data, private boards, or behavioral data are accessed at any time.

3. How Data Is Used

All retrieved data is used exclusively for internal business intelligence purposes, including trend forecasting, product assortment planning, and market gap analysis within SHEIN's merchandise operations team. Data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising targeting.

4. Data Storage & Security

Any data processed by this tool is stored exclusively on SHEIN's internal, access-controlled servers located within SHEIN's secure corporate infrastructure. Access is strictly restricted to authorized SHEIN designers and trend analysts. All data transmission is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.

5. Third-Party API Compliance

This tool integrates with third-party APIs (including the Pinterest API) strictly in accordance with each platform's Developer Terms of Service and API Usage Policy. API credentials are stored securely and are never exposed publicly. Rate limits and usage guidelines are respected at all times.

6. Retention & Deletion

Trend keyword data is retained for a maximum of 90 days for analysis purposes and is then purged from internal systems. No long-term archival of third-party platform data takes place beyond this window.
