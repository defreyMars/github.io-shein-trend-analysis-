Data Usage & Pinterest API

Our research tool utilizes the Pinterest API to analyze aggregate fashion trend data. We are committed to the following data protection principles:

  • Data Deletion: If a user chooses to disconnect their Pinterest account or revoke access to this application, all Pinterest-derived data associated with that session will be immediately and permanently deleted from our systems.
  • No Resale or Redistribution: We strictly do not resell, redistribute, or share Pinterest content or any Pinterest-derived data with any third parties.
  • Privacy First: We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII) from Pinterest users.
